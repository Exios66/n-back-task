Adaptive N-back Cognitive Task

Instructions

← Target
→ Non-target

In this cognitive task, letters will appear one at a time. Your goal is to determine if the current letter matches:

  • 0-back: The letter X
  • 1-back: The immediately previous letter
  • 2-back: The letter from two trials back

The test automatically adapts difficulty based on your performance. Respond as quickly and accurately as possible using the arrow keys or touch buttons.
